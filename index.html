Introduction

我是苏洛明（Leon Soo），一名数字产品生产者，致力于将想法和创意转化为可用的数字产品。

我的原则是专注：无论是生活还是项目，都应追求卓越与创新。

I'm Leon Soo, a digital product creator dedicated to turning ideas and creativity into practical digital experiences.

In recent years, I've mainly worked with Java and Vue to develop, test, and deploy cloud-native web products. With over a decade of team leadership experience, I take great satisfaction in guiding teams to collaborate efficiently and deliver large-scale projects successfully. 主要项目

Main Projects

我始终相信，技术的真正价值在于让人们的生活更加高效与便捷。

OpenCode（opencode.cloud） 是我独立维护的开源项目，旨在借助开源的力量，让创造力回归最纯粹的本质——探索、协作与创新。 OpenCode Util 是面向开发者的轻量级开源工具库，凝聚了我在日常开发中的经验积累，也融合了众多优秀开源项目的设计理念。希望它能帮助更多开发者以更简单的方式构建项目、创造价值。

未来，OpenCode 将继续秉持「简洁、稳定、开放」的理念，推出更多实用的工具与框架，让开发过程更加纯粹，也更富乐趣。

I have always believed that the true value of technology lies in enabling people to live more efficiently and conveniently.

OpenCode (opencode.cloud) is an open-source project I maintain independently, aiming to harness the power of open collaboration and bring creativity back to its purest essence—exploration, collaboration, and innovation. OpenCode Util is a lightweight open-source toolkit designed for developers. It embodies my years of hands-on experience and integrates the best design ideas from other open-source projects. I hope it can help more developers build projects and create value with greater simplicity.

Looking ahead, OpenCode will continue to uphold the principles of simplicity, stability, and openness, delivering more practical tools and frameworks that make development not only more streamlined—but also more enjoyable.
