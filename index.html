主要项目

Main Projects

OpenCode（opencode.cloud）是我独立维护的一个开源项目，它的前身是 Facecto.com。由于工作繁忙，早期项目一度停止维护，但我始终坚信：开源的意义不止于代码共享，更在于知识的流动与思想的交流。

OpenCode 的愿景是让更多开发者能够以更开放的方式去构建、去学习、去创造。技术的价值，在于连接与启发，而非界限与隔阂。我们所做的一切，都是希望通过开源的力量，让创造力回归最纯粹的本质 —— 自由、协作与创新。

目前我主要维护的是 OpenCode Util 系列 —— 一组面向开发者的轻量级开源工具库，旨在提升代码的可复用性与工程效率。它不仅是我在日常开发中的积累与沉淀，也是希望为社区提供一些可借鉴、可扩展的基础组件。

未来，OpenCode 将继续围绕"简洁、稳定、开放"的理念，推出更多实用的工具与框架，让开发的过程更纯粹、更有乐趣。

OpenCode (opencode.cloud) is an open-source project I maintain independently. It originated from Facecto.com, an earlier project that was discontinued due to a busy work schedule. Yet, I've always believed that the essence of open source lies not only in sharing code, but in enabling the flow of knowledge and the exchange of ideas.

The vision of OpenCode is to empower developers to build, learn, and create in a more open way. The true value of technology lies in connection and inspiration, not in boundaries or separation. Everything we do is driven by one belief: that open source brings creativity back to its purest form — freedom, collaboration, and innovation.

Currently, I'm maintaining the OpenCode Util series — a collection of lightweight open-source utility libraries designed to enhance code reusability and engineering efficiency. It represents both my personal development experience and a contribution to the community, offering practical and extensible components for developers.

In the future, OpenCode will continue to embrace the principles of simplicity, stability, and openness, delivering more useful tools and frameworks that make development cleaner, more enjoyable, and truly creative.
